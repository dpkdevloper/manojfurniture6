About

Manoj Furniture is one of best sofa manufacturer in kirti nagar whose design are amazable and unique for interior of different kind of areas like residencial and commercial. We make interior as u like or according to our clients satisfaction. we have a very hard working team of interior designer, supervisors, carpenters and more.

Owner of manoj furniture is a very respectable person of New timber market dealers association due to their hard working struggle of life as well as work. He belongs from a poor family background from bihar but his struggle in life and their work will change his life from a rented shop to the owner of the shop now Manoj furniture is very popular in kirti nagar which is one of the asia's no. 1 furniture hub.
